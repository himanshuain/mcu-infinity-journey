Space Stone
The Tesseract
1942Captain America: The First Avenger
Hidden for centuries in a church in Tønsberg, Norway behind an Asgardian mural. Red Skull and HYDRA discover it. Scientist Armin Zola uses it to power weapons. Red Skull touches it directly—the Space Stone punishes him, teleporting him to Vormir. The Tesseract falls into the Arctic Ocean. Howard Stark recovers it; it enters S.H.I.E.L.D. custody.
1995Captain Marvel
At Project Pegasus, Mar-Vell harnesses it for a light-speed engine. Carol Danvers gains powers from the blast. Goose swallows the Tesseract. Later returns to S.H.I.E.L.D..
2012The Avengers
Loki steals it and opens a wormhole over New York for the Chitauri. Thor returns it to Asgard.
2017Thor: Ragnarok
Loki grabs it from the vault during Asgard's destruction and escapes on the Statesman.
2018Avengers: Infinity War
Thanos takes it from Loki on the Statesman, crushes the cube, and adds the Space Stone to the Gauntlet.
Mind Stone
Loki's Scepter → Vision
2012The Avengers
Thanos gives Loki the scepter. Loki uses it to control Hawkeye, Selvig. Scepter goes to S.H.I.E.L.D..
2015Age of Ultron (Prelude)
Strucker/HYDRA steal it, create Wanda & Pietro in Sokovia.
2015Age of Ultron
Ultron steals it, creates synthetic body. Vision is born with the stone in his forehead.
2018Infinity War
Wanda destroys the stone (killing Vision). Thanos reverses time and takes it.

The Aether
AncientThor: The Dark World
Malekith & Dark Elves tried to use it. Bor hid it. During Convergence, Jane bonds with it. Malekith extracts it, Thor defeats him. Given to Collector on Knowhere.
2013(continued)
Malekith kills Frigga, attacks Asgard. Loki tricks him. Odin sends Aether away.
2018Infinity War
Thanos attacks Knowhere, extracts Aether from Collector, takes Reality Stone.

The Orb
2014Guardians of the Galaxy
Hidden on Morag. Quill steals it before Ronan. Collector's servant Carina vaporized touching it. Guardians defeat Ronan, give to Nova Corps on Xandar.
2018Infinity War (off-screen)
Thanos attacks Xandar, claims Power Stone—first of six.

2018Infinity War

Red Skull touched Tesseract → banished to Vormir. Becomes Stonekeeper—cannot claim stone (incapable of love).
2018Infinity War
